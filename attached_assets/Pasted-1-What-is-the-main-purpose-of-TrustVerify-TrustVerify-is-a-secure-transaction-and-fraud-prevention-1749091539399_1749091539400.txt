1. What is the main purpose of TrustVerify?
TrustVerify is a secure transaction and fraud-prevention platform designed to protect individuals and businesses from online scams and fraudulent activities during digital transactions.

It addresses a growing global problem: lack of trust between unknown buyers and sellers, freelancers and clients, or individuals on marketplaces, especially across borders.
