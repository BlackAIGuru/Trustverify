User Experience Journey
Step 1 – Discovery

Landing page for “TrustVerify Academy” with:

Course catalogue (Foundation, Intermediate, Advanced, Certification)

Pricing plans (individual vs business)

Testimonials/case studies

Step 2 – Enrollment & Access

Seamless checkout (Stripe/PayPal/Flutterwave)

Instant account creation after payment

Tier-based access (Bronze/Silver/Gold)

Single sign-on (SSO) with TrustVerify platform

Step 3 – Learning Dashboard

Personalised dashboard with:

Progress tracker (percent complete, badges)

Certificates earned

“Continue where you left off” feature

Suggested next courses

Step 4 – Learning Experience

Professionally produced video lessons (clear audio, branding, captions)

Paired handouts (download PDF directly from lesson page)

Embedded quizzes & case studies

Mobile-friendly layout (most learners will access via phone)

Step 5 – Certification & Recognition

On course completion:

Auto-issued digital certificate (PDF, blockchain verifiable)

Badge for LinkedIn/website (“TrustVerify Certified Fraud-Resilient Business”)

3. Professional Features to Include

Gamification: Badges, points, leaderboards to encourage engagement

Micro-learning: Break content into small chunks (5–10 mins each)

Accessibility: Subtitles, multi-language support (English + local markets: Yoruba, Hausa, French for Africa; Spanish for LATAM)

Feedback Loop: Post-course surveys + “rate this module”

Community Forum: Private group (LinkedIn, Slack, or in-platform) where learners can ask questions & share insights

4. Tech Stack (LMS Integration)

Option A: Embed 3rd Party LMS (fast launch)

Teachable / Thinkific / LearnWorlds / TalentLMS

White-label with TrustVerify branding

Subscription controls + analytics built-in

Option B: Build Custom LMS Inside TrustVerify

Tech: React frontend + Node.js backend + Supabase for storage + AWS S3 for video hosting

Features: role-based access, progress tracking, certificate generation

Scalable but takes more dev time

(Hybrid approach is possible: start with white-label → migrate to custom when scale justifies)

5. Professional Standards

Production Quality:

HD video with good audio, branded intro/outro

Consistent slide templates & TrustVerify colors

Real fraud case studies to make learning practical

Course Design Best Practices (ADDIE / Bloom’s Taxonomy):

Learning objectives → content → assessment → certification

Example: “By end of Module 6, learner can conduct a basic fraud risk assessment for a small business.”

6. Pricing & Access Experience

Individuals:

Easy one-off purchase with instant access

Upsell to annual subscription

Businesses:

Team dashboards (HR can monitor staff progress)

Volume discounts for >50 employees

Branded certificates for corporate use

7. Continuous Improvement

Update courses quarterly with new fraud trends (e.g. AI scams, crypto frauds)

Partner with insurers/regulators for co-branded webinars

Offer CPD (Continuing Professional Development) credits → makes it attractive to accountants, compliance officers, auditors
