TrustVerify Fraud Academy — Syllabus & Delivery Plan
1. Audience Tracks
We can split training into two main tracks:
Business Track (SMEs, corporates, financial institutions)
Individual Track (consumers, freelancers, marketplace users)
2. Detailed Syllabus
Level 1 – Foundation (Awareness & Basics)
Module 1: Understanding Fraud (types, impact, global stats)
Module 2: Online Safety for Individuals (phishing, scams, identity theft)
Module 3: Business Fraud Basics (procurement fraud, payroll fraud, invoice scams)
Module 4: Access Control & Authentication Essentials
Module 5: Reporting Fraud (how to work with Action Fraud, banks, police)
Deliverables:
Handouts: Fraud risk checklist (personal & business)
Videos: “Top 10 scams in 2025 and how to avoid them” (animated, 5 mins each)
Quizzes: 10–15 Q multiple choice
Level 2 – Intermediate (Controls & Prevention)
Module 6: Segregation of Duties & Internal Controls
Module 7: Vendor/Supplier Fraud Prevention (KYB, verification, TrustVerify API demo)
Module 8: Cyber Hygiene (password policies, MFA, endpoint security, VPN use)
Module 9: Fraud Risk Assessment Techniques for SMEs
Module 10: Data Protection & Privacy Laws (GDPR, NDPR, UK regulations, FCA rules)
Deliverables:
Handouts: Access control matrix template, vendor due diligence checklist
Videos: Real-world case study breakdowns (15 mins each)
Quizzes + scenario-based assignments
Level 3 – Advanced (Risk Management & Incident Handling)
Module 11: AML & KYC Best Practices (financial sector)
Module 12: Insider Threats & Behavioural Red Flags
Module 13: Incident Response & Evidence Gathering
Module 14: Partnering with Arbitrators & Insurers (dispute resolution, insurance tie-in)
Module 15: Fraud Analytics & Detection Tools (dashboards, anomaly detection, AI/ML basics)
Deliverables:
Handouts: Incident response playbook, red flag indicators list
Videos: Expert interview with fraud investigators (20 mins)
Assignments: Simulated fraud detection exercises
Level 4 – Expert (Leadership & Certification)
Module 16: Building a Fraud Prevention Framework (ISO 37001, COSO)
Module 17: Corporate Governance & Ethical Responsibility
Module 18: Advanced Cybercrime Trends (AI scams, deepfakes, crypto fraud)
Module 19: Insurance & Risk Transfer (fraud cover products)
Module 20: Capstone Case Study + Final Certification Exam
Deliverables:
Handouts: Fraud policy template, board-level fraud reporting framework
Videos: Live masterclass (recorded, 30–45 mins)
Certification: “TrustVerify Certified Fraud-Resilient Business / Professional”
3. Content Split: Handouts vs Videos
Handouts (PDF): Checklists, templates, policy guides → great for practical use.
Videos: Case studies, scenarios, explainer animations → high engagement.
Mixed: Quizzes, assignments, simulated fraud events.
